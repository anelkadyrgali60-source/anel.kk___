1. The Brand Persona: "The Silent Storyteller"
The persona is an Academic Creative. She is a student at Charles University or FAMU, wearing a monochrome, structural outfit, let's say a charcoal grey coat, with a single, matte silver Koshkar-muiz pendant. She doesn't need to shout to be noticed; her jewelry invites a conversation rather than a glance.

Traits: Intellectual, serene, culturally grounded, and avant-garde.

Voice: Measured and sophisticated. The language uses "we" to create a sense of belonging to a shared cultural evolution.

Vibe: An art gallery in Holešovice meets a quiet morning on the Almaty highlands.

2. Color Palette: "Tengri Minimalism"
We are using the "Eternal Sky" of Kazakh heritage but desaturating it to fit the "Old World" stone textures of Prague.

Primary Color: #B2C9D6 (Dusty Caspian)

Why: A muted, pastel blue that feels like silk and silver. It is the "modern femininity" you described—calm, pure, and airy.

Accent Color: #D1C7B7 (Silver-Birch Sand)

Why: A warm, matte neutral that mimics the "matte silver" finish of the jewelry and provides a soft, editorial backdrop for the blue.

3. Hero Section Welcome Message
"Heritage, Redefined in Silence."
Discover ethno-minimalism born from the Great Steppe and curated for the heart of Prague. We transform ancient Kazakh symbols into modern silver talismans—for a generation that carries history with grace
